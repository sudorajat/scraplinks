About the Founder

Yonir Jain a 16 year old student of Springdales School, Dhaul kuan came across the idea of a waste management startup in 2019 when he saw the Ghazipur Landfill in the outskirts of Delhi. India has a population of 1.38 billion and faces massive waste management challenges–inefficient waste infrastructure, lack of planning, and increasing rate of solid waste generation per capita. Understanding this particular concern he used his entrepreneurial skills and gave rise to a unique and innovative business solution that cures the environment in a profitable way. While researching deeply on his new startup idea, he encountered with a disturbing truth of the waste industry - the kabadiwalas. Usually when people hear the wordKabadiwala the picture of a financially deprived individual wearing worn out sandals and a torn shirt diving in a pool of garbage comes in their mind. People regard this profession as dirty and undignified. Aiming to change the people’s perspective towards the waste business, Yonir gave rise to Scraplinks that not only aims to help its clients earn a buck through their waste but also to provide the local kabadiwalas and scrap pickers a livelihood and a dignified life along with proper and hygienic working conditions.

Mission and vision:

The company’s main objective is to provide its customers a platform where they can convert their trash into valuable resources. Our main goal is to ease our customers by providing them a doorstep service that would collect their trash and also pay for its diminishing value.

  • Our company aims to create a network of kabadiwala by linking and collaborating with local and intermediate kabadiwalas. By doing this we would help solve the problem of exploitation that is faced by many kabadiwalas by providing them with secure jobs and stable income.

  • Proper system of weights and measurement: we would offer an accurate system of weight and measure to our customer for measuring the weight of their trash.

  • Better and Competitive pricing: we would be providing our customers the best rates for their trash which would be comparatively 5% more than the average market price.

  • Trained and experience staff: Inorder to provide our customers a no complaint service we would have a team of trained and experienced staff who will take care of even the minutest details for our valuable customers

  • Hygienic and easy to call service: One can simply call or contact us through our app or website and book a hygienic and safe meeting for selling or donating their trash.

  • Quick and hassle free service & immediate payment upon receipt.
